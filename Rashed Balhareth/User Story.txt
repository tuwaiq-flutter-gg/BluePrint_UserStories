

User Story:

User: Parents

-as a parent, we want to log in using email and password to enter the application.

-as a parent, we want to view the places and activities in the entertainment for our children.

-as a parent, we want to view the description of the place.


-as a parent, we want to save the places we like on our favorite page


-as a parent, we want to share the places we like with our friends and family, so that we can meet them at our favorite places

-as a parent, we want to schedule the activity on the calender 


as a parent, we want to book an activity ticket
